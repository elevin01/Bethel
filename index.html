What is Bethel?

Bethel was not built—it was discovered. A moment where God met a person, and a place became holy ground. The name means “House of God.”

“Surely the Lord is in this place, and I did not know it.” Genesis 28:16

Our prayer is simple: that every visitor would find an encounter that transforms, a community that strengthens, and a calling that sends.
